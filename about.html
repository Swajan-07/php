About PUBG Mobile India

PUBG Mobile India is a battle royale game that has taken the gaming world by storm. Developed by PUBG Corporation, it offers an immersive and thrilling experience for players. The game features various modes, including solo, duo, and squad, where players can team up with friends or go solo to be the last one standing.
